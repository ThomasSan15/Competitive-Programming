Contest
---
Contest are "competitions" where are (usually) 6 problems, in increasing difficulty.

we can increase or decrase our rank in Codeforce, ChefCode, Leetcode, etc with the contest, if we obtain the necessary rating we increase our rank, otherwise we decrease it.

**The divisions have the next categories:**

**Div 4 <= 1400**

**Div 3 <= 1600**

**Div 2 <= 2100**

**Div 1 <= 2800**
