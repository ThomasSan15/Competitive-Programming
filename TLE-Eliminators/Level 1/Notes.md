Contest
---
The contest are "competitions" where are (usually) 6 problems, in incresing level form.
we can increase or decrase our rank in Codeforce, ChefCode, Leetcode, etc with the contest, if we obtain the necessary puntuation, we increase our rank, otherwise we decrease it.

**The div´s have the next categories:**

**Div 4 <= 1400**

**Div 3 <= 1600**

**Div 2 <= 2100**

**Div 1 <= 2800**
